Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow vour taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions. tart cranberries, and creamv brie. It will become vour new favorite holidav starter!

Ingredients:

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups (500) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desidered, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium-size mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togrthrt all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
